Scrambled Eggs

Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 TBSP Butter
  • 2 TBSP Cream
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Place eggs in a bowl and mix in cream
  2. Melt butter in a non-stick pan on low heat
  3. Once the pan is fully heated, pur the egg mixture in. Allow it to cook until a layer forms on the bottom of the pan.
  4. After this, begin slowly stirring to keep eggs from sticking to the bottom of the pan
